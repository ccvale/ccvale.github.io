My name is Chris Valente, a senior Computer Science major at Ramapo College, also minoring in Data Science. In that facet, I've written countless scripts ranging in scale and complexity, from automating a web-scraper to send me a daily schedule of interesting events, to implementing song recognition API to manipulate my lights to match the colors of the album of a song it hears. My biggest interest at the moment is in working with raw data, and turning it into a story that explains a narrative.
